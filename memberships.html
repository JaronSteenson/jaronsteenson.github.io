Further info

Casual hourly hireage

Casual hourly hireage of courts is $20 per hour (for non members). This is done by registering to Hello Club and selecting “Visitor” as the booking type.

There is no hireage of equipment available however we do have some rackets available to be borrowed on site.

Unlimited

This category may use all club facilities at any time of the day and night, and additionally has the right to play in district Interclub competitions and approved tournaments.

Tertiary

Tertiary members receive the same benefits as Unlimited members but must be full time students up to the age of 25 years. A valid full time student identity card must be shown at time of joining.

Casual Monthly

No minimum time commitment (must give two weeks notice to end).

Junior

Junior members must be 18 years old or younger. Junior members can play between the following hours:
7:00 AM – 5:30 PM weekdays
7:00 AM – 2:00 PM weekends and public holidays

Country

Country members must provide proof of club affiliation at another club and will be able to make use of courts excluding during peak times midweek (4:30-7:30pm).

Family (2 unlimited and 2 junior)

The parent members of the family may use the courts at any time of the day and night, and additionally have the right to play in district Interclub competitions and approved tournaments.
